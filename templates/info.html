Some learners aren’t given the tools they need, not because they’re incapable, but because the system wasn’t designed with minds like theirs in mind.

BrightWired is a toolkit built for neurodivergent learners who think differently and learn differently. Instead of forcing brains to fit into conventional study methods, BrightWired does the opposite. It adapts to you. Because neurodivergence doesn’t need to be fixed. It isn’t a flaw. It’s a different perspective — one that deserves to be understood and designed for.

Who It's For

BrightWired is meant for students who feel that traditional study methods are unreliable, overwhelming or simply unsuited to how they learn — whether they’re dyslexic, have ADHD or just learn in a way that’s different.

Features at a Glance

  • Distraction-free reading mode and UI
  • Customizable font and color settings
  • Timers with gentle reminders for scheduled tasks
  • Neuro - friendly planner
  • Summarisation Tools
